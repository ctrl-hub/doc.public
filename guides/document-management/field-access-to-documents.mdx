## What You'll Accomplish

Following this guide, you'll be able to:

- Access CDM documents directly from field operations
- Download documents to your mobile device for offline reference

---

## Prerequisites

- You have access to Ctrl Hub with field user permissions
- You have the mobile app installed on your device or are accessing the web portal
- You understand your current operation and work assignment
- You have appropriate permissions to view CDM documents for your assigned operations
- Your device has sufficient storage to download and cache documents (recommend 100MB+ available)
- You have a basic understanding of your organisation's document structure and naming conventions

## Tips & Best Practices

<Tip>Familiarise yourself with the document structure before arriving on site. Knowing what documents to expect helps you work more efficiently when you need to access them in the field.</Tip>

<Tip>Download critical CDM documents to your device before starting work, especially in areas with poor connectivity. This ensures you always have access to essential safety information.</Tip>

<Tip>When annotating documents, be specific with your observations. Include locations, measurements, photos, and clear descriptions so the project team understands your findings.</Tip>

<Tip>Always review the latest version of a document before starting work on a task. Check the approval status to ensure you're working from the most current and approved version.</Tip>

<Tip>Take timestamped photos of your work in relation to CDM plans. This creates visual evidence that work was completed according to approved designs and safety plans.</Tip>

<Tip>When you encounter a discrepancy between a document and actual site conditions, annotate the document and notify your supervisor immediately. This helps catch and resolve issues quickly.</Tip>

<Tip>Use the acknowledgment feature for all critical safety documents. This creates a record that you've reviewed important information and understand your responsibilities.</Tip>

<Tip>When downloading documents for offline use, ensure your device has sufficient storage and battery. Check storage space before downloading large files or multiple documents.</Tip>

<Tip>Mark completed document-based tasks promptly with sign-offs. This helps project managers track progress and ensures compliance records are up to date.</Tip>

<Tip>If you notice outdated or incorrect information in a document, report it to your supervisor. Outdated documentation can create safety and quality issues on site.</Tip>

<Warning>Always work from the latest approved version of a document. Using outdated versions can lead to rework and safety issues.</Warning>

<Warning>Document annotations are shared with the project team. Be professional and factual in your comments and markups.</Warning>

## Troubleshooting

| Issue                                       | Solution                                                                                                                                       |
| ------------------------------------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Cannot see Documents tab in my operation    | Ensure the operation has documents assigned to it. Contact your project manager to request document sharing if needed.                         |
| No documents appear in my work area         | Documents may be filtered by phase or operation type. Check that your current operation matches the document assignments.                      |
| Search is not finding documents             | Try using different keywords. Documents are tagged by type and phase. Use broader search terms if specific searches return no results.         |
| Document won't download                     | Check your device's available storage space and internet connection. Try downloading a smaller document first to test.                         |
| Annotations aren't saving                   | Ensure you have a stable internet connection to sync annotations. If the connection drops, annotations may not save. Try again once connected. |
| Cannot view previous versions               | Not all documents have version history. The document must have multiple versions available before previous versions can be viewed.             |
| Acknowledgment failed to submit             | Check your internet connection. Acknowledgments require connectivity to sync with the server. Try submitting again when connection is stable.  |
| Approval status shows as pending            | The document may still be in review by approvers. Check back later or contact your supervisor for approval status.                             |
| Photos attached to documents aren't visible | Photos may take time to upload and sync. Refresh the document view to see recently added photos.                                               |
| Cannot access a document I'm assigned to    | Verify your permissions for the operation and scheme. Contact your project manager if you believe you should have access.                      |
| Document appears corrupted or won't open    | Try downloading the document again. If the problem persists, contact your supervisor. The original file may need to be re-uploaded.            |
