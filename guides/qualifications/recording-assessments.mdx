## Step-by-Step guide

### Record a completed assessment

1. Navigate to **People > Qualifications** and select the **Awards** tab
2. Find the qualification and holder that requires an assessment
3. Select **Update** to open the award window
4. In the **Assessments** panel, set the assessment date and time
5. Leave the **Result** as **Passed** (or select the appropriate result)
6. Select **Save** to record the assessment
7. The assessment will now appear in the qualification record with the date, time, and name of the person who recorded it
8. The qualification holder's status may change from "Awarded, not yet passed assessment" to "Valid" once a passed assessment is recorded
