## Overview

This guide explains how to install and use the Ctrl Hub mobile app to access your assigned operations. You'll learn how to view your daily operations, start and manage form submissions, and handle offline submissions when connectivity is unavailable.

---

## What You'll Accomplish

Following this guide, you'll be able to:

- Install the Ctrl Hub mobile app on Android or Apple devices
- Log in to the app and access your assigned operations
- View your daily operations and full operation list
- Start form submissions on assigned operations
- Save and continue draft submissions
- Create and submit form submissions while offline
- Understand how Ctrl Hub handles form states (Pending, Submitted, Published)
- Review and track submissions in the Ctrl Hub web platform

---

## Prerequisites

- You have access to Ctrl Hub and have been assigned at least one operation
- You have an Android or Apple mobile device
- You have downloaded and installed the Ctrl Hub app from Google Play Store (Android) or Apple App Store (iOS)
- Your assigned operations have required forms configured (such as a Service Card form)
- You have your Ctrl Hub login credentials (email and password) readily available
- Your mobile device has sufficient storage space for the app and form submissions
- Your device meets minimum operating system requirements (iOS 12.0+ or Android 8.0+)
- You understand that operations are created from work orders in your assigned schemes
- You are familiar with your device's offline capabilities and data storage
- You understand the form submission workflow and what information will be collected
- You have internet connectivity for initial app setup and account authentication

---

## Step-by-Step Guide

---

### Install and Log In to the Ctrl Hub Mobile App

1. On your Android or Apple device, open the Google Play Store (Android) or Apple App Store (iOS)
2. Search for "Ctrl Hub" and select the official app
3. Select **Install** and wait for the app to download and install
4. Once installed, open the Ctrl Hub app
5. Follow the login prompts to authenticate using your Ctrl Hub credentials
6. After successful login, you'll be directed to the home page

### View Your Assigned Operations

1. On the home page, you'll see **Today's Operations** - operations scheduled for today's date
2. To view all your assigned operations, select **VIEW ALL**
3. You'll see a complete list of all operations assigned to you
4. Select **Operations** from the bottom toolbar to access the full Operations page at any time
5. Your assigned operations are listed with their current status

### Start a Form Submission

1. From your list of operations, select the operation where you want to start a form submission
2. When the operation page opens, view the **Associated Forms** section
3. Select the form you want to complete (for example, "SGN Service Card")
4. Begin populating the form fields with your data
5. Use the **Next** button to move between form pages
6. Fill in all required fields as you progress through the form

### Save Your Draft Submission

1. While filling out a form, if you need to pause before completion, press the **Home** button
2. Your progress is automatically saved as a draft
3. The submission will appear in the **Recent Draft Form Submissions** section on your home page
4. You can return to the submission at any time to continue where you left off

### Continue and Complete a Draft Submission

1. On the home page, locate **Recent Draft Form Submissions**
2. Select the draft submission you want to continue
3. Use the **Next** button to navigate through the submission to where you left off
4. Continue filling in the remaining required fields
5. When complete, select **Submit**
6. If prompted, review your form using **Previous** and **Next** buttons to identify and fill any empty required fields
7. Select **Submit** to finalise the submission

### Create Submissions While Offline

1. Enable airplane mode on your device or turn off Wi-Fi and mobile data
2. Select an operation from your Operations list
3. Select the required form (such as "SGN Service Card") from the Associated Forms section
4. Fill in the form completely, using the **Next** button to move between pages
5. When finished, select **Submit**
6. Fill in any empty required fields if prompted
7. Select **Submit** again
8. The submission will be saved as "Pending" - awaiting a data connection for final submission

### Submit Offline Submissions When Connectivity Returns

1. Turn on your data connection (disable airplane mode or reconnect Wi-Fi/mobile data)
2. Open the Ctrl Hub app
3. Select **Forms** from the bottom toolbar
4. Select the **Submitted** tab to view your pending submissions
5. Wait a few moments for the app to attempt to submit any pending forms
6. Check the **Published** tab - your submission should now appear there with a status of "Published"
7. If your submission still appears as "Submitted", open it and select **Submit** again to retry

### Review Submissions in the Web Platform

1. Go to **Governance > Schemes** in the Ctrl Hub web platform
2. Select a scheme to view its details
3. On the Scheme page below the map, select the **Submissions** tab
4. View all submissions for all Work Orders and Operations under that scheme
5. Alternatively, select a specific Work Order and go to its **Submissions** tab to view submissions for only that work order
6. Review submission details, statuses, and metadata for compliance tracking

## Expected Results

After following these steps, you should have:

- Successfully installed and logged into the Ctrl Hub mobile app
- Access to all your assigned operations via the Operations page
- The ability to view daily operations on your home page
- Completed form submissions appearing in the Published list
- Draft submissions saved and available for continuation
- Understanding of offline functionality and how pending submissions are handled
- Successfully submitted forms that appear as "Published" in the Forms section
- Access to the web platform for viewing and tracking all scheme submissions
- The ability to filter submissions by Work Order for detailed analysis and compliance tracking

## Tips & Best Practices

<Tip>On your home page, **Today's Operations** shows all operations scheduled for the current date. Use **VIEW ALL** to access operations scheduled for future dates.</Tip>

<Tip>Drafts are automatically saved when you return to the home page. You don't need to manually save your progress - just press Home and your submission will be saved as a draft.</Tip>

<Tip>When working offline, you can continue to create and submit forms. Ctrl Hub will automatically send them once you regain internet connectivity.</Tip>

<Tip>Use the **Previous** button to review your form responses before submitting. This helps you catch errors and ensure all information is accurate before final submission.</Tip>

<Tip>
  On poor network connections, allow extra time for submissions to sync. Ctrl Hub will retry automatically, but you can also manually trigger submission by opening the pending form and selecting
  **Submit** again.
</Tip>

<Tip>Monitor your device's battery level during long form-filling sessions. Consider using a power bank if you'll be working offline for extended periods.</Tip>

<Tip>Keep your app updated to the latest version to ensure you have all features and security patches. Check Google Play or Apple App Store regularly for updates.</Tip>

<Tip>If you have multiple schemes or work orders assigned, check the top of the Operations page to see which scheme/work order context you're currently viewing.</Tip>

<Tip>Pending offline submissions persist on your device until successfully submitted. Don't clear app data or uninstall the app until all pending submissions are removed.</Tip>

<Warning>Offline submissions are held as "Pending" until a data connection is available. Don't uninstall the app or clear its data while pending submissions exist - they may be lost.</Warning>

<Warning>Always fill in all required fields before submitting. The app will prompt you if any required fields are empty, and you won't be able to submit until they're completed.</Warning>

## Troubleshooting

| Issue                                                   | Solution                                                                                                                                                                            |
| ------------------------------------------------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Operations don't appear on home page                    | Ensure the operations are scheduled for today's date. Use VIEW ALL to see operations scheduled for other dates.                                                                     |
| Login credentials not accepted                          | Verify you're using the same credentials as your web account. Contact your administrator if you've forgotten your password.                                                         |
| Draft submission doesn't appear                         | Check the Recent Draft Form Submissions section on your home page. If still missing, refresh the app or log out and log back in.                                                    |
| Cannot submit while offline                             | Create your submission in offline mode and it will be automatically submitted when connectivity returns. No manual action needed.                                                   |
| Submission stuck as "Pending" after reconnecting        | Wait a few moments for the app to sync. If still pending, open the form and select Submit again to retry the submission process.                                                    |
| Required fields still show empty after filling          | Ensure you've used the Next button to navigate through all form pages. Some required fields may be on subsequent pages.                                                             |
| App crashes when opening an operation                   | Close the app completely and reopen it. If the problem persists, try logging out and logging back in.                                                                               |
| No associated forms appear for an operation             | The operation may not have forms assigned. Contact your administrator or check the web interface to confirm the operation's configuration.                                          |
| Cannot find Submissions tab in Governance               | Ensure you've selected a scheme first. The Submissions tab only appears within a scheme's detail page, not at the Governance level.                                                 |
| Submissions don't appear in web platform                | Submissions may still be pending sync from the mobile app. Wait a few moments and refresh the page. Ensure the submissions have been published in the mobile app.                   |
| App won't download or install                           | Ensure you have sufficient storage space on your device. Check device compatibility (iOS 12.0+ or Android 8.0+). Try restarting your device and attempting install again.           |
| "Insufficient storage" error during app use             | Free up space on your device by deleting unnecessary files or apps. Offline submissions require temporary storage space. Consider moving photos/videos to cloud storage.            |
| Form pages won't load or timeout                        | Check your internet connection. Wait a moment and refresh the form. If problem persists, close and reopen the app. Ensure the operation has forms properly configured.              |
| Field validation errors preventing submission           | Ensure all required fields are filled with valid data. Check for field-specific requirements (email format, phone number format, date format, etc.). Use Previous button to review. |
| Previous/Next buttons not responding                    | Close the form and reopen it. Ensure all required fields on the current page are filled before attempting to move to the next page. Some fields may be conditional.                 |
| Unable to switch between tabs (Today/All/Forms)         | Refresh the app by closing and reopening it. Log out and log back in if tabs remain unresponsive. Ensure app is fully updated to the latest version.                                |
| App logs me out unexpectedly                            | Session timeout is a security feature. Log back in. If this occurs frequently, check your device's app background activity settings - the app may be stopping unexpectedly.         |
| Offline submission sync is taking too long              | Large file submissions or slow connections may take several minutes to sync. Leave the app open and connected to a network. Check your internet speed.                              |
| Operations appear but show as "Inaccessible"            | The operation may have restrictions based on your user role or permissions. Check with your administrator. You may not have been assigned to the operation correctly.               |
| Form fields disappear or reset to blank                 | This may occur if the app loses connection during form entry. Save frequently using the Home button to create draft checkpoints. Resume from your last draft.                       |
| Cannot find specific operation by name                  | Use the Operations list and scroll to find the operation. Try viewing operations scheduled for different dates using the date filter. Check with your administrator if missing.     |
| Offline mode won't enable or airplane mode incompatible | Ensure airplane mode doesn't disable automatic syncing features needed. You can work offline by simply not having internet connection without enabling airplane mode.               |
| Connectivity issues during submission                   | Connection interrupted during sync may leave submission as Pending. Reconnect and manually trigger retry by opening the form and selecting Submit again.                            |
| Draft submissions taking too long to save               | Auto-save may be slow on weak connections. Use the Home button to force save before the app times out. Check device storage space availability.                                     |
| Operations list shows duplicate entries                 | Refresh the Operations page. This sometimes occurs with network sync issues. Log out and log back in to resync your operation list.                                                 |
| Form keyboard won't close after typing                  | Tap elsewhere on the form outside the text field. Some form fields may keep keyboard open until you move to another field. Try tapping the Next button.                             |
| Character limit error when filling field                | Individual form fields have character limits set by your administrator. Ensure your entry doesn't exceed the limit (typically shown as "X/250 characters" etc.).                    |
| Cannot attach images or files to form                   | Your form may not support attachments. Check if the field is an attachment field. Ensure file is below size limit (typically 10MB). Try JPG format for images.                      |
| Location/GPS field won't populate                       | Enable location services in your device settings. Ensure the app has permission to access location. Allow a moment for GPS to acquire signal in outdoor areas.                      |
| Date/time picker shows incorrect format                 | Your device's locale settings determine date/time format. Return to your operation list and check today's date to understand the correct locale format for your device.             |
