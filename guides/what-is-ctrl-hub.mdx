## What is Ctrl Hub?

Ctrl Hub is a comprehensive management platform designed to streamline project governance, ensure regulatory compliance, and enable efficient field operations. Whether you're managing CDM documentation, tracking workforce qualifications, or capturing data in the field, Ctrl Hub provides the tools you need to keep projects running smoothly and safely.

---

## Key Capabilities

### Document Management for CDM Packs

Maintain complete control over your Construction (Design and Management) documentation with advanced approval workflows and version control.

**What you can do:**

- Upload and organise document files with folder structures
- Request approvals and comments from stakeholders
- Track document approval history and changes
- Set deadlines and expiry dates for critical documentation
- Download documents for offline review
- Move, rename, and delete documents and folders as needed
- Manage new document versions with full change history
- Add additional approvers to approval workflows
- Receive notifications about document updates

### Qualification and Competence Management

Ensure your workforce meets all necessary qualifications and compliance requirements with comprehensive competence tracking.

**What you can do:**

- Assign qualifications to users based on job roles
- Upload and view certificates for verification
- Track qualification expiry and renewal schedules
- Record successful assessments and training dates
- Set awarded dates for newly obtained qualifications
- Monitor risk status and non-compliance reporting
- Verify holder qualifications
- View complete qualification history
- Create custom qualifications for specific requirements
- Manage shared qualifications across multiple projects
- Define assessment requirements for different roles

### Project Governance and Contractor Sharing

Organise your construction projects, create work packages, and seamlessly share information with contractors and stakeholders.

**What you can do:**

- Create and manage projects with structured governance
- Define operations and work orders for organised project execution
- Assign operations to operatives and track progress
- Share projects with contractors and external teams
- Access shared projects from partner organisations
- Update work progress in real-time
- Create bridges between different organisations for secure sharing

### Offline-First Field Data Capture

Capture critical field data even without internet connectivity, with powerful form building and mobile-ready submission workflows.

**What you can do:**

- Build custom forms with various field types
- Structure forms with steps and transitions for complex workflows
- Create submissions linked to projects or ad-hoc activities
- Work offline in the field with automatic data sync
- Find and manage submissions for your assigned operations
- Track project-specific and ad-hoc submissions
- Publish submissions when ready for review
- Apply validation and constraints to ensure data quality
- Monitor mobile submission statuses in real-time

### Customer Service Management

Manage customer relationships effectively with integrated tools for account management, interaction tracking, and scheduling.

**What you can do:**

**Customer Accounts & Contact Details**

- Create and maintain customer accounts with contact information
- Store multiple contact points (email, phone, physical address)

**Interactions & Contact Attempts**

- Log customer interactions (telephone calls, emails, letters)
- Track contact attempts with timestamps and outcomes

**Appointment Booking**

- Schedule appointments with customers directly in the platform
- Assign engineers to field operations
