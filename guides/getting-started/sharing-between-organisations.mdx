## Overview

This guide explains how to access the Ctrl Hub SaaS platform, manage your login credentials, and find organisation members. You'll learn how to log in, reset a forgotten password, change your password, and search the members list.

---

## What You'll Accomplish

Following this guide, you'll be able to:

- Access the Ctrl Hub web console from any browser
- Recover a forgotten password using a recovery code
- Change your password while logged in
- Search the members list to find colleagues and organisation members
- Understand how to manage your account security

## Prerequisites

- You have an email address registered in the Ctrl Hub system
- You have access to your email account (required for password recovery)
- You have a modern web browser installed on your device (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge)
- JavaScript is enabled in your browser (required for login functionality)
- Cookies are enabled in your browser (required to maintain login sessions)
- You understand your organisation's password requirements (complexity, length, special characters)
- You understand that password recovery codes are temporary and expire after a set period
- For searching members, you have access to the People section (may require specific permissions)
- You know your email address as registered in the Ctrl Hub system exactly as it was entered

### Expected Results

After following these steps, you should be able to:

- Successfully log into the Ctrl Hub console
- Access your organisation's data and features
- Reset your password if forgotten
- Change your password whenever needed
- Find colleague and organisation member information quickly
- Manage your account security independently

<Tip>Bookmark **https://console.ctrl-hub.com** in your web browser for quick access. You can also save it as a home page to make logging in even faster.</Tip>

<Tip>When resetting a forgotten password, check your spam or junk email folder if you don't see the Recovery Code email within a few minutes.</Tip>

<Tip>Create a strong password that includes uppercase letters, lowercase letters, numbers, and special characters. This improves your account security.</Tip>

<Tip>When searching the members list, you can search by first name, last name, or full email address. Try different search terms if your first attempt doesn't find the person you're looking for.</Tip>

<Tip>Use a password manager (such as 1Password, LastPass, or Dashlane) to securely store your Ctrl Hub password. This eliminates the need to remember complex passwords.</Tip>

<Tip>Recovery codes are temporary and typically expire within 24 hours. Complete the password reset process immediately after receiving the code.</Tip>

<Tip>After changing your password, clear your browser cache and cookies to ensure your new password is properly registered for your next login.</Tip>

<Tip>The profile icon location in the top right corner may vary slightly depending on your screen size or device. Look for a circle with initials or a small avatar image.</Tip>

<Tip>Member search is case-insensitive, so searching for "john" will find "John" or "JOHN". Use this to your advantage when you're unsure of name capitalization.</Tip>

<Tip>Your new password takes effect immediately after saving. Use it on your next login attempt, not your old password.</Tip>

<Warning>Never share your password with anyone, including Ctrl Hub support staff. They will never ask for your password.</Warning>

<Warning>If you don't receive a password recovery email within 5-10 minutes, contact your system administrator. The email address may not be registered in the system.</Warning>

## Troubleshooting

| Issue                                                       | Solution                                                                                                                                                    |
| ----------------------------------------------------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| "Invalid email or password" error                           | Verify you're using the correct email address and password. Check for typos and ensure Caps Lock is off.                                                    |
| Password recovery email not received                        | Check your spam and junk email folders. Verify the email address is correct and registered in the system. Wait 5-10 minutes before requesting another code. |
| Forgot the Recovery Code                                    | Request a new password recovery code by starting the password reset process again.                                                                          |
| Cannot log in after changing password                       | Ensure you're using the new password, not the old one. Clear your browser cache and try again.                                                              |
| Account locked after multiple login attempts                | Wait 15-30 minutes before trying again. Contact your administrator if the account remains locked.                                                           |
| Member search returns no results                            | Try searching with different name variations or the member's email address. Verify the person is actually a member of your organisation.                    |
| Cannot access the People > Members section                  | Verify you have the appropriate permissions to view the members list. Contact your administrator if needed.                                                 |
| Profile icon not visible in top right corner                | Ensure you're fully logged in. Refresh the page or try a different browser.                                                                                 |
| Browser shows "Connection refused" or "Cannot reach server" | Verify you've entered the correct URL: **https://console.ctrl-hub.com** (with the "s" in https). Check your internet connection.                            |
| JavaScript error or page doesn't load fully                 | Enable JavaScript in your browser settings. Ctrl Hub requires JavaScript to function properly.                                                              |
| Cookies disabled error message                              | Enable cookies in your browser settings. Ctrl Hub uses cookies to maintain your login session.                                                              |
| Recovery code has expired                                   | Recovery codes typically expire after 24 hours. Request a new password recovery code by starting the process again.                                         |
| Password field appears empty or masked                      | This is normal - password fields hide characters for security. Type your password even if you can't see it.                                                 |
| New password was rejected or failed to save                 | Ensure your new password meets your organisation's requirements (length, special characters, etc.). Your new password must be different from the old one.   |
| Login works but page shows loading indefinitely             | Wait 30 seconds for the dashboard to fully load. If it continues, clear your browser cache and try logging in again.                                        |
| Session expires or logged out unexpectedly                  | Log back in. Session timeout is a security measure. Longer sessions are available through your administrator settings.                                      |
| Multiple browser tabs or windows causing issues             | Log out from all other windows and clear your cache. Then log in again in a single window.                                                                  |
| Email field rejects your email address                      | Verify the email address matches exactly how it was registered (case-insensitive for domain, but may matter for local part). Check for extra spaces.        |
| Password recovery code won't accept input                   | Ensure you've copied the entire code from the email without extra spaces. Try pasting it rather than typing manually.                                       |
| Member search is very slow                                  | Large organisations may have slower search performance. Wait for results or try a more specific search term.                                                |
| Cannot change password due to permission error              | Contact your administrator. Password changes may be restricted by security policies in your organisation.                                                   |
| Bookmark of console.ctrl-hub.com shows error                | Delete the bookmark and recreate it. Sometimes cached bookmark data can cause issues. Type the URL fresh.                                                   |
