## Step-by-Step Guide

### Search the Members List

1. Log in to the Ctrl Hub console
2. Navigate to **People > Members** from the main menu
3. You'll see a list of all members in your organisation
4. In the search field at the top of the list, enter a member's name or email address
5. The list will automatically filter to show only members matching your search criteria
6. You can search for partial names (e.g., "John" will find "John Smith" and "Johnny Jones")
7. Select a member from the filtered results to view their profile
8. Clear the search field to return to the full members list
