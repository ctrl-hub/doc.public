## Step-by-Step Guide

### Access the Ctrl Hub web console

1. Open your web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge)
2. Navigate to **https://console.ctrl-hub.com**
3. You'll be directed to the Ctrl Hub login page
4. Enter your registered email address in the email field
5. Enter your password in the password field
6. Select **Log In**
7. You'll be authenticated and logged into the Ctrl Hub console
8. You'll see your home dashboard with access to all platform features

### Download the Ctrl Hub mobile app

For Apple iPhone and iPad users

1. On your mobile device, open the App Store

For Android phone or tablet users

1. On your mobile device, open the Google Play Store
