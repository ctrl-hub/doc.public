## Step-by-Step Guide

### Recover a Forgotten Password

1. Go to **https://console.ctrl-hub.com** in your web browser
2. On the login page, select **Forgot your password?**
3. Enter the email address associated with your Ctrl Hub account
4. Select **Continue**
5. Check your email inbox for a message from Ctrl Hub containing a temporary Recovery Code
6. Copy the Recovery Code from the email
7. Return to the password recovery page and enter the Recovery Code
8. Follow the prompts to create a new password
9. Confirm your new password
10. Once completed, you can log in using your email address and new password

### Change Your Password

1. Log in to **https://console.ctrl-hub.com** with your current credentials
2. In the top right corner of the page, select your profile icon (a circle with your initials or avatar)
3. From the dropdown menu, select **Profile**
4. On your Profile page, select **Change Password**
5. Enter your current password to verify your identity
6. Enter your new password in the **New Password** field
7. Confirm your new password by entering it again
8. Select **Update Password** or **Save** to apply the change
9. Your password has been successfully changed
10. On your next login, use your new password to access Ctrl Hub
