Schemes are the data schemes in your forms. They define the structure and constraints of the form data. When we talk about schemes, we are talking about the shape of the data that you want to capture.

They are a machine readable representation of the form data - we use [JSON Schema](https://json-schema.org) as the standard representation format, which is a widely adopted and well-documented specification.
