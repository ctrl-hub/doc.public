---
title: 'Templates'
---

Templates are a way to create a blueprint for a scheme, work order or operation. They can be used to quickly create new resources with a predefined structure.

Schemes and Work Orders can control the labels which are applied to the resources that are created from them.

Operations also control labels, but they also allow you to control the required and optional forms which can be completed against the operation. This becomes important for automating the completeness of your projects.
