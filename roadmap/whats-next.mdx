## Submission Workflows <Badge color="green">Launching soon</Badge>

<Card highlight="success">**Streamlined Data Collection** — A redesigned submission workflow engine that simplifies how teams capture, validate, and process field data.</Card>

### What to Expect

Submission workflows will bring a range of improvements to the data collection experience:

- **Smart Form Progression** — Dynamic form sequences that adapt based on user responses, showing only relevant fields and reducing unnecessary data entry
- **Real-time Validation** — Immediate feedback on field entries during submission, with clear guidance on corrections needed before submission
- **Conditional Logic** — Advanced rules that automatically show or hide form sections based on previous answers, creating a more intuitive experience
- **Multi-step Tracking** — Clear visual indicators showing submission progress, current step, and completion percentage
- **Batch Submission Support** — Process multiple submissions in a single operation with template application across records
- **Workflow State Persistence** — Save progress on incomplete submissions and resume exactly where you left off, even when offline

### Why This Matters

<Card highlight="info">
  Field teams spend significant time managing complex data collection workflows. By streamlining how submissions are created and processed, we're reducing administrative overhead and improving data
  quality at the source.
</Card>

## Consolidated Competence Non-Compliance Reporting <Badge color="green">Launching soon</Badge>

<Card highlight="success">**Unified Compliance Insights** — A comprehensive reporting dashboard that aggregates competence and qualification non-compliance across your entire organisation.</Card>

### What to Expect

This new reporting capability will provide:

- **Executive Summary Reports** — High-level overview of compliance status across teams, projects, and qualifications with key metrics and trends
- **Drill-Down Analytics** — Navigate from summary statistics to individual non-compliance cases with full audit trails and remediation history
- **Automated Alerts** — Proactive notifications when competence gaps are identified, with recommended remediation actions before they impact operations
- **Custom Report Builder** — Create and schedule reports filtered by project, team, qualification type, or compliance status
- **Export Capabilities** — Generate PDF, Excel, or CSV reports for stakeholder communication and regulatory compliance documentation
- **Trend Analysis** — Historical tracking of compliance improvements over time with benchmarking against organisational targets

### Visual Dashboard Features

The reporting dashboard will include:

<Card>
  - **Compliance Heatmaps** showing risk levels across teams - **Non-compliance Timeline Charts** for trend visualisation - **Drill-down Lists** for individual case investigation - **Remediation
  Status Tracking** to monitor corrective actions
</Card>

### Why This Matters

<Card highlight="info">
  Scattered non-compliance data makes it difficult to identify patterns and take preventive action. A consolidated view enables better resource allocation, faster remediation, and better governance
  reporting.
</Card>

## Property Data with MPRN, MSN, and PSR Yes/No Flags <Badge color="blue">In development</Badge>

<Card highlight="success">**Enhanced Property Intelligence** — Richer property data attributes that provide more context for site assessment and compliance workflows.</Card>

### What to Expect

Properties will soon include additional utility and service identification data:

- **MPRN (Meter Point Reference Number)** — Gas supply identification for properties connected to gas utilities, enabling precise utility-specific compliance checks
- **MSN (Meter Serial Number)** — Electric meter identification for improved electrical safety assessments and utility coordination
- **PSR Flags (Problem Solved Record)** — Yes/No indicators showing whether previous compliance or service issues have been resolved, reducing duplicate work
- **Data Integration** — Automatic population of these fields from utility databases or manual entry with validation
- **Searchable Properties** — Enhanced property search using these new identifiers to quickly locate records across projects

### Integration Benefits

<Card>
  These enhanced properties enable: - Better correlation with utility company records - Clearer history of resolved vs. outstanding compliance issues - Faster property identification in large
  portfolios - Reduced duplicate assessments and rework
</Card>

### Why This Matters

<Card highlight="info">
  Construction and building safety workflows require precise property identification and history tracking. These utility and compliance identifiers bridge the gap between site-level work and broader
  utility and regulatory systems.
</Card>

## Field Access to CDM Documents <Badge color="blue">In development</Badge>

<Card highlight="success">**Documents in Your Pocket** — Direct access to Construction Design and Management documentation from the field, integrated with project workflows.</Card>

### What to Expect

Field teams will be able to:

- **View CDM Resources** — Access construction phase plans, health and safety documents, and risk assessments directly within the mobile and web applications
- **In-Context Access** — See relevant documents filtered to the current operation, phase, or work area without manual searching
- **Annotation Support** — Mark up documents with observations or issues found during field work, with timestamps and location data
- **Digital Sign-Off** — Acknowledge document receipt and completion of document-based tasks with tamper-proof audit trails
- **Search and Filtering** — Find specific documents by type, date, phase, or custom tags
- **Version Control** — Always access the latest approved document versions with clear indicators of updates and changes

### Field Workflow Integration

<Card>Documents link seamlessly with: - Daily operations and task lists - Mobile form submissions - Safety checklists - Work order progress tracking</Card>

### Why This Matters

<Card highlight="info">
  CDM regulations require that construction workers have access to relevant health and safety documentation. Field access ensures compliance while improving safety awareness and reducing coordination
  friction on site.
</Card>

## Offline Storage for CDM Documents <Badge color="blue">In development</Badge>

<Card highlight="success">**Always Available** — Critical CDM documents cached locally on devices, accessible even when connectivity is lost.</Card>

### What to Expect

The offline document storage feature will provide:

- **Smart Caching** — Automatically download relevant CDM documents to field devices before leaving the office, based on assigned operations and work areas
- **Sync Management** — Efficient synchronisation that checks for document updates and intelligently manages device storage to avoid bloat
- **Offline Availability** — Full document access (view, search, annotate) when working in areas with unreliable connectivity
- **Automated Updates** — When connectivity is restored, new and updated documents are automatically refreshed on the device
- **Storage Optimisation** — Intelligent compression and cleanup of old documents to maintain device performance
- **Data Security** — Encrypted local storage with remote wipe capabilities if devices are lost or compromised

### Sync Intelligence Features

<Card>
  - Selective document download based on project phase - Incremental sync for faster updates - Background synchronisation when on WiFi or charging - Conflict resolution for concurrent document updates
</Card>

### Why This Matters

<Card highlight="info">
  Field sites often have spotty connectivity. Having critical documentation available offline ensures work isn't delayed by connectivity issues while maintaining full document access and compliance
  requirements.
</Card>
