## Mobile App Enhancements

<Card highlight="info">**Status:** Exploring possibilities for major mobile experience improvements</Card>

We're thinking about ways to make the mobile app even more powerful for field teams:

- **Enhanced Offline Capabilities** — More robust offline functionality with advanced caching strategies
- **Biometric Authentication** — Fingerprint or face recognition for faster, more secure login
- **Improved Form Templates** — Better visual design and usability for complex forms on small screens
- **Barcode/QR Code Integration** — Quick scanning of work orders, properties, or documents to speed up workflows
- **Push Notifications** — Real-time alerts for urgent work items, approvals, and validation issues

### Questions We're Exploring

- How much offline functionality do teams actually need?
- Are there specific form types that are difficult to use on mobile today?
- Would barcode scanning solve current workflow friction points?

## Advanced Reporting & Analytics

<Card highlight="info">**Status:** Evaluating data visualisation and insights capabilities</Card>

We're considering tools to help teams get more insights from their operational data:

- **Custom Dashboard Builder** — Allow users to create personalised dashboards with drag-and-drop widgets
- **Predictive Analytics** — Use historical data to forecast completion dates, resource needs, or compliance risks
- **Comparative Reporting** — Compare performance across teams, projects, or time periods
- **Data Export Enhancements** — Improved ways to export and analyse data in external tools
- **Real-time Metrics** — Live dashboards showing current operation status, team performance, and KPIs

### Questions We're Exploring

- What metrics matter most for your organisation's decision-making?
- How frequently do you need to access reporting data?
- Would automation of report generation save your team time?

## AI-Assisted Features

<Card highlight="info">**Status:** Researching AI/ML capabilities that could add value</Card>

As AI tools mature, we're exploring how they could enhance Ctrl Hub workflows:

- **Intelligent Form Suggestions** — AI-powered suggestions for form field values based on context and history
- **Automated Quality Checks** — Machine learning models to flag potentially incomplete or inconsistent submissions before they're completed
- **Smart Document Analysis** — Extracting key information from uploaded documents and pre-populating form fields
- **Compliance Risk Prediction** — Identifying teams or projects at risk of non-compliance before issues arise
- **Natural Language Search** — Search documents and records using conversational queries instead of exact field matches

### Questions We're Exploring

- What manual data entry tasks take the most time on your team?
- How comfortable are you with AI-powered suggestions in critical workflows?
- Are there specific compliance checks that would benefit from automation?

## Integration & Ecosystem

<Card highlight="info">**Status:** Evaluating partnerships and API expansion possibilities</Card>

We're thinking about how Ctrl Hub can integrate more deeply with the systems you already use:

- **Calendar Integration** — Sync operations and deadlines with Google Calendar, Outlook, or Microsoft Teams
- **Messaging Platform Integration** — Receive notifications and respond to approvals through Slack or Microsoft Teams
- **Project Management Sync** — Two-way sync with tools like Jira, Monday.com, or Asana

- **Third-party Authentication** — Support for SAML, OAuth2, or other enterprise authentication providers

### Questions We're Exploring

- Which external systems are most critical to your operations?
- How do you currently bridge information between Ctrl Hub and other tools?
- What integration would remove the most manual work from your processes?

## Field Experience Innovations

<Card highlight="info">**Status:** Exploring ways to make field operations more efficient</Card>

We're thinking about features that could fundamentally improve how teams work on site:

- **Augmented Reality Support** — AR overlays for viewing documents, seeing hidden utilities, or navigating complex sites
- **Voice-Powered Input** — Complete forms and log observations using voice instead of typing
- **Photo Annotation Tools** — Mark up photos with measurements, arrows, or text for documentation
- **Environmental Sensing** — Automatic logging of equipment readings, temperature, or other sensor data
- **Proximity-Based Workflows** — Automatic task switching or form display based on location on site

### Questions We're Exploring

- How much time do teams spend typing on dusty, wet, or cold job sites?
- Would visual documentation (annotated photos) reduce the need for written reports?
- Are there high-risk areas on sites where AR safety features would help?

## Process Automation

<Card highlight="info">**Status:** Researching workflow automation engine possibilities</Card>

We're considering how to help teams automate repetitive processes:

- **Workflow Automation Engine** — Create workflows that trigger actions based on form submissions, approvals, or status changes
- **Scheduled Operations** — Automatically create recurring operations or reminders
- **Approval Workflows** — Complex approval chains with conditional routing based on submission content
- **Data Transformation** — Automatically format, validate, or reformat data between systems
- **Bulk Actions** — Perform operations on multiple records simultaneously with templates

### Questions We're Exploring

- What manual processes do you repeat frequently?
- How complex are your current approval workflows?
- Are there data entry tasks that follow predictable patterns?

## User Experience Improvements

<Card highlight="info">**Status:** Gathering feedback on daily usability</Card>

We're continuously thinking about quality-of-life improvements:

- **Configurable Dashboards** — Let teams customise what they see on their dashboard

### Questions We're Exploring

- What daily tasks feel like they take longer than they should?

- What customisation options would make the platform work better for your specific workflows?
